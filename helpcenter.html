How do I open a membership account?
We're happy to hear you want to join the FOCUS EYEFIT Club! Opening an account is really simple;After you place your order with us, we automatically create an account and send you an activation email.

Where are you located?
We’ll ship your order from one of our 4 logistics facilities around the world depending on where the product is originally sourced. Our goal is to get the product to you as quickly and efficiently as possible!

Do I have to pay any custom duties?
No way! That’s on us. If in the unlikely event that your parcel is held by customs and you are notified you have to pay additional customs duties, we will 100% fully reimburse the costs. All you need to do is request a formal invoice once you have paid, and submit a copy to our customer services team so that we can sort out your reimbursement.

How do I find my order number?
After placing your order you will receive an email within 24 hours that lists your unique order number. It will consist of two letters, followed by 10 numerical digits, and then one or two more letters. So, it will look something like this: IN0123456789G.

Where is my order?
As soon as your order is shipped, you will receive an email with the tracking information so that you can know where your purchase is at any moment.

What is your Return Policy?
Let's face it — buying from the internet can be difficult and time-consuming. Therefore, we've introduced a hassle-free process which includes a 100 days return policy for your order (prescription lenses are also included).

Our friendly service team is here to help you during our regular business hours.
At Focus Eyefit, we value our customers and ensure that you can find the answers you are looking for!
